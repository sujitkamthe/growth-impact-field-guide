## Mindset

"I can deliver high-quality work independently and understand the context around it."

**The question you're trusted to answer:** "What's the right way to build this?"

## Nature of Impact

- Individual → local team
- Feature-level ownership
- Craft excellence emerges

## Success Looks Like

- Strong engineering fundamentals
- Reliable delivery
- Beginning to influence solutions, not just implement them

---

# Technical Delivery

## Expectations

- Independently implements stories end-to-end
- Breaks down stories into logical implementation steps
- Writes clean, readable, and maintainable code
- Applies SOLID principles and common design patterns where appropriate
- Writes unit and integration tests by default
- Uses meaningful naming and structure
- Debugs functional, integration, and environment issues independently
- Works across backend, frontend, data pipelines, and infra as needed
- Understands service boundaries and data flows
- Reviews peers' PRs with specific, actionable feedback
- Refactors code opportunistically while delivering features
- Understands basic performance bottlenecks
- Uses logs, metrics, and alerts effectively
- Follows secure coding practices
- Keeps implementation aligned with agreed design and acceptance criteria
- Takes responsibility for defects introduced by their code

## Self-Assessment

- Would someone unfamiliar with the code understand it easily?
- Do I improve code quality even when not explicitly asked?
- Can I explain why I chose this implementation?
- Can I implement a full story end-to-end without needing someone to unblock me?
- Do I catch my own bugs before they reach code review?

---

# Consulting

## Expectations

- Asks "why" behind stories, not just "how"
- Identifies gaps, contradictions, or missing requirements
- Understands basic business and domain context
- Suggests incremental improvements to proposed solutions
- Flags ambiguity proactively to team or leads
- Understands constraints such as timelines, cost, or regulations
- Connects individual stories to broader features
- Engages stakeholders with support
- Begins to think in trade-offs

## Self-Assessment

- Do I challenge unclear asks respectfully?
- Can I articulate the business problem, not just the ticket?
- Do I surface ambiguity early?
- Do I suggest alternatives when requirements seem off, or just implement what's asked?
- Can I explain the trade-offs behind my recommended approach?

# Delivery Excellence

## Expectations

- Owns stories end-to-end
- Estimates work reasonably
- Delivers predictably within commitments
- Tracks progress transparently
- Flags risks early
- Understands dependencies
- Participates actively in planning and retrospectives
- Adjusts plans when reality changes
- Maintains sustainable pace

## Self-Assessment

- Can others depend on my commitments?
- Do I manage my work or react to it?
- Do I flag risks before they become blockers?
- Are my estimates reasonably accurate, or do I consistently over- or under-commit?
- Do I adjust my plan when things change, or wait for someone else to redirect me?

---

# Mentorship & Talent Growth

## Expectations

- Helps onboard new team members
- Shares knowledge generously
- Gives basic, constructive feedback
- Explains reasoning, not just answers
- Creates a welcoming environment

## Self-Assessment

- Do others feel safe asking me for help?
- Do I invest time in helping others learn?
- When I help someone, do I explain the reasoning or just give them the answer?
- Do newer teammates come to me for guidance without being directed to?
- Have I helped someone avoid a mistake by sharing what I learned?

---

# Communication & Influence

## Expectations

- Articulates ideas clearly
- Writes clear documentation
- Communicates progress and blockers proactively
- Adapts communication style to audience
- Summarises discussions accurately

## Self-Assessment

- Can I explain my thinking to non-technical audiences?
- Do my messages reduce confusion?
- Do I write documentation that others actually use?
- Do I proactively share progress, or do people have to ask me?
- When I disagree, can I articulate my position without creating friction?
