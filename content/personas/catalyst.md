## Mindset

"I actively improve outcomes by shaping solutions, influencing people, and unblocking systems."

**The question you're trusted to answer:** "What should we build and why?"

## Nature of Impact

- Team-level
- Problem-space ownership
- Visible consulting behavior

## Success Looks Like

- Teams move faster and clearer because of you
- Ambiguity reduces when you engage
- Others trust your judgment

---

# Technical Delivery

## Expectations

- Designs solutions for complex, ambiguous, or cross-cutting requirements
- Breaks down large problem spaces into coherent technical approaches
- Anticipates edge cases, failure modes, and operational risks
- Makes deliberate architectural and design trade-offs
- Evaluates build vs buy decisions
- Leads technical discussions and design reviews
- Documents designs and rationale clearly
- Improves test strategy (not just coverage)
- Introduces better abstractions when needed
- Spots and addresses technical debt early
- Improves system observability and debuggability
- Considers performance, cost, and reliability together
- Guides others through complex debugging
- Aligns technical decisions with long-term system health
- Coaches others on technical reasoning, not just solutions

## Self-Assessment

- Do people seek my input for non-trivial technical decisions?
- Do I proactively prevent problems rather than react to them?
- Am I improving the system beyond my assigned scope?
- Have I designed a solution that others built on top of without needing my involvement?
- Do I identify technical debt before it becomes a crisis?

# Consulting

## Expectations

- Drives problem framing discussions end-to-end
- Reframes vague asks into clear, solvable problem statements
- Separates symptoms from root causes
- Proposes multiple solution options
- Clearly articulates trade-offs (tech, cost, risk, time)
- Helps stakeholders make informed decisions
- Pushes back when asks are misaligned or risky
- Reduces ambiguity for the entire team
- Handles conflicting stakeholder priorities
- Acts as a thought partner, not an order-taker
- Maintains trust while challenging assumptions
- Owns solutioning outcomes

## Self-Assessment

- Do stakeholders come to me for thinking or only for updates?
- Do discussions become clearer because I'm involved?
- Can I handle disagreement without escalation?
- Have I reframed a vague ask into a clear problem statement that the team acted on?
- Do I push back on misaligned requirements, or accept them to avoid conflict?

# Delivery Excellence

## Expectations

- Owns epics or milestones
- Manages cross-team dependencies
- Identifies and mitigates delivery risks proactively
- Makes scope, time, and quality trade-offs explicit
- Improves delivery flow and predictability
- Champions agile principles, not just rituals
- Aligns delivery with business priorities
- Balances speed with quality and sustainability

## Self-Assessment

- Do I improve how work flows through the system?
- Do I make trade-offs visible and explicit?
- Have I resolved a cross-team dependency without escalating it?
- Do I anticipate delivery risks before they materialise?
- Has the team's predictability improved because of changes I introduced?

# Mentorship & Talent Growth

## Expectations

- Mentors consistently over time
- Helps others develop independent judgment
- Resolves conflicts constructively
- Improves team dynamics
- Supports onboarding and ramp-up effectively
- Gives timely, actionable feedback

## Self-Assessment

- Have people grown because of my support?
- Do I invest in others' growth proactively?
- Can I point to someone whose judgment improved because of my mentoring?
- Do I give feedback that changes behaviour, not just feedback that's heard?
- Am I helping people develop independence, or creating dependence on me?

# Communication & Influence

## Expectations

- Drives discussions to decisions
- Facilitates meetings and workshops effectively
- Summarises options, decisions, and next steps
- Handles disagreement constructively
- Influences through clarity and reasoning

## Self-Assessment

- Do discussions move forward because of me?
- Can I handle tough conversations calmly?
- Do I facilitate meetings that end with clear decisions and next steps?
- Do people seek me out to help resolve disagreements?
- Can I influence a decision without relying on authority or escalation?
